Primary School

Like many others, I enrolled into a nearby Primary School in their neighbourhood.

The school I enrolled into is Compassvale Primary School, and I went through all 6 years of formal Primary School education.

It was both a blissful and also a stressful place to be in, due to many opportunities to interact with classmates while also keeping up with studies.

I also joined a Soccer Club in my 3rd year of Primary School, and I remember how gruelling the physical training were because of the lack of consistent fitness activities in that time.

While I did not fully excel in Primary School, I was also not doing too badly while picking up subjects in Primary School.

At the end of Primary School, I accumulated a score of 163 for my PSLE year end examination and landed myself on a Normal Academic stream in Secondary School.

Secondary School

After Primary School, I enrolled into Sengkang Secondary School for my high school studies in 2018.

During Secondary School, I have made many long-lasting friendships and accquaintances as a Normal Academic student. It was also this time I joined the school's media club, which is in-charge of the school's performances and media productions.

I had a lot of fun in the first few years of my youth, and also came to learn many meaningful life lessons there.

This was also the time where I developed my interests, beliefs, values and identity as a person. This is especially so in the 3rd year of Secondary School, where responsibilities are became more serious and life-changing.

At last, after years of studying and hardwork, I was able to enter into the Polytechnic Foundation Programme. This programme meant that I could enter into a Polytechnic early and reserve a spot in the course of my choosing.

Nanyang Polytechnic

As of now, I am currently enrolled into Nanyang Polytechnic for my diploma studies from 2022 to expected 2025.

I am now in the School of Design & Media sector, majoring in Game Development & Technology.

Throughout the learning journey in Polytechnic, I have learnt many new languages and software which helped me to create and design my projects.

These software I have learnt thus far are: Microsoft Visual Studios 2022, Visual Studios Code, Unity Engine, Unreal Engine, and Android Studios.

My current Academic Achievements and standing are: Director's List (Fall 2023 & Spring 2024).

Current GPA: 3.79 / 4.00.
