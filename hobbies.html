Gangnam Company

Gangnam Company is a single-player, 3D horror survival game and is a remake of the original Lethal Company game from Zeekers.

The game is created entirely in Microsoft Visual Studios 2022, using C++ Language as a base framework, OpenGL as its graphics library, and irrKlang for music and sound effects under a two-week schedule. It is developed by a group of 4 members including me under a two-week schedule.

I was in-charge of creating the Collision System, Sound System, Shaders, Scene Manager, and Game User Interface (GUI/UX) design.

In this game, players must collect and sell valuable items to fulfill a quota whilst surviving on 1 of 2 planets by gathering materials to craft items to prolong their survival under a time limit.

Super Mario Remix

Super Mario Remix is a single-player, 2D Puzzle Platformer and a re-imagination of the original Super Mario Bros game.

The game is created entirely in Microsoft Visual Studios 2022, using C++ Language as a base framework, OpenGL as its graphics library, irrKlang for sound effects, and is made under a two-week schedule by myself.

In this game, players can choose two different gamemodes: Puzzle or Arena.

Puzzle gamemode requires the player to parkour from one platform to another and solve different puzzles in the world along the way.

Arena gamemode requires the player to fight against a set amount of enemies and make their way to the end of the map.

Gui's Arcade

Gui's Arcade is a single-player, 2D Arcade-style simulation game where players can choose between three mini-games to play in: Racing, Survival & Parkour.

The game is created entirely in Unity Engine 2022, using C# Language and its built-in engine features. It is made under a two-week schedule by myself.

In racing, players can drive a car and utilise boost pads and race themselves in a race-track.

In survival, players can control a tank, shoot bouncing projectiles & rockets, displace ragdolls and drive around in the wilderness.

In parkour, players can jump and move between different platforms, while trying not to fall down the map.

Lone Knight

Lone Knight is a single-player, 2D Metrovania-style game where players have to fight against enemies, progress through worlds, and defeat the final boss of the game.

The game is created entirely in Unity Engine 2022, using C# Language and its built-in engine features. It is made under a two-week schedule by myself.

In this game, players can pick-up weapons & items in the map, such as a sword or a bow to fend off against hostile enemies present in the map.

Players can also traverse across the map using a wide-range of abilities available, such as a dash or a grappling hook. At the end of the game, the player has to fight against a difficult boss with multiple phases.

Apex Predator

Apex Predator is a single-player, 2D Action RPG game where players have a choice to either help the village by killing animals or choose to follow the tribe by capturing animals and prevent endangered animals from going extinct.

The game is created entirely in Unity Engine 2022, using C# Language and its built-in engine features. It is developed by a group of 4 members including me under a two-week schedule.

I was in-charge of developing the inventory system, Artificial Intelligence (AI) enemies, and Quest system.

In this game, players can choose two routes to complete the game: the village quest route or the tribal quest route. The village quest route requires players to continously kill animals, while the tribe quest route encourages players to protect endangered animals by capturing them.

Both routes would result in an ending of the game when the environment is either irreparably damaged forever or the environment is protected and flourished.
